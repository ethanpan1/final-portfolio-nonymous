About Me

Hi! My name is Ethan, and I’m a junior at Brown University majoring in Cognitive Science and Food Writing. I have worked as a Data Science Intern at Kaiyo and as a TA for Intro to Linguistics and Data and Society. You can download my resume here.

I’m currently looking for full-time user research internships for Summer 2021.

My research always aims to center human experiences while maintaining a strong grounding in quantitative analysis. I am passionate about projects that expand social good, especially when they relate to cultural mediators like language and food. In my free time, I sing, play guitar, and write.

In my work, I always prioritize communication and collaboration. I truly believe that learning about and with others is the key to life. So, if you would ever like to talk, please reach out at my email. Can't wait to meet you!
